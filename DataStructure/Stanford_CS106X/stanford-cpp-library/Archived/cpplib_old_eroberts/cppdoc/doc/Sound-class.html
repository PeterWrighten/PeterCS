class Sound

This class encapsulates a sound file, which is typically specified in the constructor.

Constructor

Sound()
Sound(filename) 		Creates a Sound object.

Methods

play() Plays the sound synchronously and waits for the sound to finish before returning.
start() Starts playing the sound asynchronously.
stop() Stops playing the sound.

Constructor detail

Sound();
Sound(string filename);
Creates a Sound object. The default constructor creates an empty sound that cannot be played. The second form initializes the sound by reading in the contents of the specified file.

Usage:

Sound sound;
Sound sound(filename);

Method detail

void play();
Plays the sound synchronously and waits for the sound to finish before returning.

Usage:

sound.play();

void start();
Starts playing the sound asynchronously.

Usage:

sound.start();

void stop();
Stops playing the sound.

Usage:

sound.stop();
